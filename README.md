## How to use

Adding a new paper:

If paper belongs to Maltas Lab or is canonical to our understanding/lab thinking:

"Please ingest raw/papers/[filename].pdf

This is a lab paper (lab_paper: true) and a bootstrapping paper (canonical: true). 
After ingesting, update lab/overview.md to reflect what this paper reveals about 
the lab's systems, methods, and research questions."

Else:

Please ingest raw/papers/[filename].pdf

lab_paper: false, canonical: false, by default.


Linting:

"Please run a lint pass on the wiki."

Linting too early might produce poor signals/results. Only lint after a section has 10-15+ papers. 
Afterward lint after 5-10 contributions.

Early on it may be helpful to use:

"Please lint the collateral-sensitivity topic hub specifically."

So you don't get false signals on stuff we haven't uploaded yet.
